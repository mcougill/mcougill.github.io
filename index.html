About Me

Hey there! I'm Molly. Although a native of the Portland, Oregon, 7 years ago I was yearning for 100 percent humidity summers and ridiculously flat terrain so I packed up and moved to HTX for college. I haven't looked back since ♥. I received a degree from Rice University (go Owls!) in Materials Science and Engineering. Just like Madonna, I'm a material girl. When I'm not busy perfecting my shavasana or exploring the Houston craft beer scene, I enjoy travelling and spending time with my friends and family. I have a kitty named Tyga and a yorkie named Moose who also hog a lot of my attention.
